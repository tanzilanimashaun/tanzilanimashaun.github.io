About Me

Hi! My name is Tanzil and welcome to my website. I’m an enthusiastic and hardworking physics graduate with the aspiration of a career in web/software development. I am particularly interested in these fields because I have a strong passion for solving mathematical/analytical problems and would like the opportunity to apply my skill-set within a business environment.

I first discovered my passion towards programming when I was introduced to computational physics and the programming language, Python. Over the duration of my degree, I learnt how to extract information from large data-sets and create modelling functions to analyse unique peaks or relevant trends. Although this was initially out of my comfort zone, I thoroughly enjoyed being introduced to new technologies as it opened my perception towards the world of software development.

It was not long before I took matters into my own hands and began teaching myself the basics of web development. For me, this was the perfect starting point. Learning HTML and CSS gave me a better understanding of how individual bits of code come together to create something useful. The simplicity, yet, effectiveness, of these mark-up languages truly fascinated me. At this point I knew that software development was the field for me. It provides a never-ending capacity to learn and improve myself whilst allowing me to be of use to society.

Within the field of software development, I am particularly interested in SDET roles as I believe this is an excellent way to develop the skills required for writing exceptional programs. First and foremost a good SDET is at heart an engineer! They have knowledge of all relevant coding practices, design patterns and an engineering mindset. In addition, an SDET role requires a curious and investigative mindset, forcing you to think outside of the box when creating versatile testing scenarios.

I am applying for the web development programme at Founders & Coders because I believe this is a great opportunity for me to further my skills as a programmer as well as collaborate with like-minded people. I am a strong believer in learning through collaboration as it brings diverse skill-sets together where everyone can learn from each other. Whether you’re helping others or being helped by others, there is always capacity to improve yourself when working in a team. Founders & Coders is one of the few organisations that provide this unique collaborative experience, which is why this is the bootcamp for me!

Thank you in advance for your consideration, and I am keenly awaiting your reply. Please do not hesitate to contact me via email for any further information.
